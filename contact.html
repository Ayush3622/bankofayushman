General Inquiries

For specific questions or to send us a message, you can reach out via email directly. Please provide as much detail as possible.

How to send us a message:

  1. Prepare your email with all necessary details (your name, account number if applicable, nature of inquiry).
  2. Send it to support@mybank.com.
  3. Our team will review your message and get back to you as soon as possible.

Please do not include sensitive information like full account passwords in your initial email. Our support team will guide you through secure verification processes if needed.
