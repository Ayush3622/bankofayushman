About MyBank: Rooted in Trust, Driven by Innovation

Discover our journey, our values, and the dedicated people behind MyBank, committed to empowering your financial well-being in India and beyond.

Ayushman Chakraborty

Founder & CEO, MyBank

"My vision for MyBank was born from a deep understanding of the financial needs of our community in Kolkata. I wanted to create a bank that blends modern technology with a personal touch, a place where individuals and businesses feel truly supported in achieving their financial aspirations. Our Journey: A Legacy of Growth and Trust

2010: The Inception

MyBank was founded in the heart of Kolkata by Ayushman Chakraborty with a small, dedicated team. Our ambition was to redefine banking in the region, focusing on accessibility and genuine customer care for local communities.

2015: Embracing Digital Transformation

Recognizing evolving customer needs, MyBank invested significantly in digital infrastructure, launching our intuitive online banking platform to provide greater convenience and accessibility.

2020: Expanding Services and Reach

We broadened our range to include robust business banking solutions, recognizing Kolkata's vibrant entrepreneurial spirit. This expansion marked our deeper commitment to the local economy.

Present: Innovation for the Future

Today, MyBank continues to innovate, investing in cutting-edge technologies for secure, convenient, and efficient banking. We remain deeply rooted in our community, guided by Ayushman Chakraborty's vision.
