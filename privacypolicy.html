MyBank Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information.

Last Updated: June 19, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes information you provide directly, such as your name, address, email, phone number, financial details (e.g., account numbers, transaction history), and identification documents. We also collect data automatically through your use of our services, including IP addresses, device information, browser type, and usage patterns.

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII) like name, address, date of birth.
  • Financial Information, including account details, transaction records, and credit history.
  • Technical Data such as IP address, device type, browser, and operating system.
  • Usage Data, including how you interact with our website and mobile applications.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is primarily used to deliver our banking services, process transactions, and manage your accounts. We also use it to enhance security, prevent fraud, personalize your experience, and communicate with you about our products and services. Occasionally, we use data for research and analytics to improve our offerings.

  • To provide and maintain our banking services.
  • To process your transactions and manage your accounts.
  • To enhance security and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To personalize your experience and offer relevant products.
  • For internal analytics, research, and service improvement.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

MyBank does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business (e.g., payment processors, IT support), subject to strict confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in response to a valid legal request.

  • With service providers for operational purposes (e.g., cloud hosting, customer support platforms).
  • With regulatory bodies and law enforcement when legally required.
  • In cases of mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, with appropriate safeguards.
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes.

4. Data Security

We employ robust security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure socket layer (SSL) technology, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We regularly review our security practices to ensure your data remains safe.

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing activities. You can also withdraw your consent at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

  • Access and review your personal data.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent for specific data uses.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze site usage, and support marketing efforts. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy (dummy link).

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.
